TYPES OF FRENCH VERBS

Er ending verbs, Ir ending verbs and Re ending verbs are used commonly in french language. The verbs that ends with er and re are infinitive. Er ending verbs belong to the first conjucation.The verbs that ends with ir belongs to the second conjucation and they talk about something done presently. The verbs that ends with re belongs to the third conjucation. Re ending verbs are the smallest category of french verbs.
